Organization Task Tracker

Introduction

It is compliance management software for organizations. Our mission is to simplify the compliance process for both administrators and front-line workers. This software makes it easy for people to follow the rules and policies in an organization. It just gives you clear, step-by-step instructions.

Purpose

The purpose of this software is to help organizations manage their tasks and compliance requirements. It helps organizations to track their tasks and ensure that they are compliant with the rules and regulations.

Features

  • Task Management
  • Compliance Management
  • Document Management
  • Reporting
